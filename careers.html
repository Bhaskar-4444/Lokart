About Vegras

At Vegras, we are redefining the grocery and raw material supply industry in New Delhi, delivering premium quality vegetables, fruits, dairy, spices, and pantry essentials to hotels, mess, paying guest (PG) accommodations, restaurants, and individual customers. Our mission is to provide fresh, organic, and reliable products with exceptional service, fostering a sustainable and innovative food ecosystem. Join us to be part of a dynamic team dedicated to quality, growth, and customer satisfaction.

Why Work with Us?

We believe in creating a workplace where creativity, collaboration, and passion thrive. When you join Vegras, you’ll enjoy:

  • Growth Opportunities: Build a rewarding career with opportunities for professional development and advancement.
  • Inclusive Culture: Be part of a diverse, supportive team that values every individual’s contribution.
  • Impactful Work: Contribute to delivering high-quality food supplies that empower businesses and households.
  • Competitive Benefits: Enjoy competitive salaries, health benefits, and a positive work environment.
  • Sustainability Focus: Work with a company committed to eco-friendly practices and community support.

How to Apply

We welcome applications from passionate individuals who share our commitment to quality and service. To apply:

  1. Send your resume and a cover letter to careers.vegras@gmail.com.
  2. Include the job title or department you’re applying for in the subject line (e.g., “Application: Customer Support”).
  3. Provide a brief introduction about yourself and why you want to join Vegras.
  4. Attach any relevant documents (e.g., portfolio for marketing roles, certifications for supply chain roles).

Our HR team will review your application and contact you within 7-10 business days if your profile matches our requirements. Due to the high volume of applications, we may not respond to all submissions.

Our Hiring Process

Our hiring process is designed to be transparent and efficient:

  • Application Review: We evaluate your resume and cover letter for relevant skills and experience.
  • Interview: Shortlisted candidates are invited for interviews, which may be conducted in-person, via phone, or video call.
  • Assessment: Depending on the role, you may be asked to complete a task or assessment (e.g., logistics planning, customer query handling).
  • Offer: Successful candidates receive a job offer with details on salary, benefits, and start date.
